LFP Spectrogram Viewier (SGView)

An LFP Spectrogram Viewer for the Bedside

New DBS hardware lets us record from the implanted DBS lead. Being able to quickly assess the quality of the recordings at the patient’s bedside is crucial to maximize the signal-to-noise in the recordings. I developed a MATLAB based UI for LFP spectrogram assessment.
